Your delivery is scheduled—thank you for your payment!

Reminder: your card is authorized only; the final charge will be processed after fueling is complete.

What Happens Next

  • You’ll receive a confirmation text shortly detailing your delivery.
  • Two hours before your fueling window, your driver will message you with their exact arrival time.
  • You’ll get periodic updates as the driver approaches and when they arrive.

Vehicle Preparation

  • Please ensure your vehicle is unlocked and the gas cap is accessible.
  • Drivers wait up to 5 minutes; after that, the service fee is non‑refundable.

After Fueling

  • The driver will lock your vehicle and secure the gas cap.
  • A paper ticket showing the exact gallons dispensed will be printed and left on the driver‑side dashboard.
  • Your final payment will be processed. Note: At the time of fueling, subscribers only pay for the cost of the gas.
  • You’ll receive an emailed receipt and a notification that fueling is finished.
