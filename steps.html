How Gas Me Up Works

Order fuel directly from the app — choose a one-time refill or subscribe for service-fee savings. Pick your fuel grade and time window, and a Gas Me Up driver will come to your vehicle and refuel it safely.

Step-by-step
1

Sign up and choose your plan

Create your account, add your vehicle, and choose between a subscriber plan or a one-time refill.

  • Subscriber: service fee is waived on eligible subscriber orders.
  • One-time refill: includes a $15 service fee added to your order total.
  • Add your vehicle details (make/model) and confirm the correct fuel type.
  • Add a payment method so checkout is quick and secure.
2

Select fuel grade and schedule your delivery

Pick your fuel grade and a time window that works for you (same-day or scheduled windows as available).

  • Choose your fuel grade (Regular/Mid/Premium) based on your vehicle’s recommendation.
  • Confirm the delivery location and drop a map pin if the area is large (lots/garages).
  • Add location notes (spot number, nearby business, entrance, gate code, etc.).
  • Ensure it’s a safe/legal spot for a quick service stop during your selected window.
3

Driver is assigned and dispatched

Once your order is confirmed, we assign a Gas Me Up driver and dispatch them to your vehicle.

  • You’ll receive status updates when a driver is assigned and en route.
  • Clear notes help us find your vehicle faster and complete service on time.
  • If access is blocked or the vehicle can’t be located, we may contact you through the app.
4

Make the gas cap area accessible

To keep things fast and hands-off, your fuel door and gas cap area must be unlocked and reachable.

  • Ensure your fuel door is unlocked (some vehicles lock the fuel door with the doors).
  • If your car uses an interior release, open the fuel door ahead of time.
  • Keep the area clear of obstacles (ice/snow, tight parking, cargo blocking access).
  • If the cap/door is not accessible, service may be delayed or require rescheduling.
5

We refuel, then secure your gas cap

Your driver confirms the correct vehicle and fuel grade, refuels your car, then secures everything.

  • Driver verifies vehicle + fuel grade before beginning service.
  • Refueling is performed carefully and professionally.
  • After completion, your driver will secure the gas cap and close the fuel door when applicable.
6

Final payment is processed

After fueling is complete, we process the final total using your payment method on file.

  • Your final total reflects the fuel delivered plus applicable fees.
  • Subscribers: service fee is waived automatically.
  • One-time refills: include the $15 service fee.
  • You’ll receive confirmation once payment is successful and the order is completed.
7

Completion notification + digital receipt

We notify you when the job is done and provide a digital receipt for your records.

  • Get a completion alert when service is finished.
  • Receive a digital receipt with key order details and total paid.
  • Receipts remain available for easy tracking and support if needed.

Quick prep checklist

  • Location details matter

    Add a spot number, nearby landmark, or access instructions so your driver finds your vehicle fast.

  • Unlock the fuel door

    Some vehicles lock the fuel door with the doors. Make sure it’s unlocked during your window.

  • Keep the gas cap area accessible

    Clear snow/ice and avoid parking too close to walls so the driver can safely reach the cap.

  • Save receipts automatically

    You’ll receive a digital receipt after completion for personal or business tracking.
