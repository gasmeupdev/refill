How It Works

Gas Me Up brings fuel directly to your vehicle. Place an order in the app, pick your fuel and time window, and our driver arrives to refuel your car safely and conveniently.

Before you place your first order
  • Confirm your vehicle details (make/model + fuel type) so we deliver exactly what you need.
  • Double-check the refuel location (parking lot, driveway, street spot) and any notes that help us find you.
  • Ensure your gas door/cap area is accessible during the selected window.
1) Sign up (Subscriber or One-Time Refill) Start here
Create your account and choose how you want to refuel: subscribe for ongoing convenience or place a one-time order.
  • One-Time Refill: Includes a $15 service fee added to the order total.
  • Subscriber: The $15 service fee is waived for subscriber orders (best value for frequent refuels).
  • Add your vehicle and confirm your fuel type (Regular/Mid/Premium).
  • Add your payment method in the app so checkout is quick and secure.
  • Optional: Add special access notes (gate code, “parked behind building,” “blue sedan in spot #22,” etc.).
2) Select fuel type + choose your time window Scheduling
Tell us what fuel you need and when you want it delivered. We’ll show available windows and help you lock in a time.
  • Select your fuel grade (87 / 89 / 91-93) based on your vehicle’s recommended octane.
  • Choose your refuel time window so you don’t have to wait around.
  • Confirm the vehicle location and add clear instructions for finding it (lot name, nearest entrance, etc.).
  • Make sure the location is safe and legal for a quick service stop during your selected window.
3) Driver assigned + dispatched to your location Dispatch
Once your order is confirmed, we assign a Gas Me Up driver and dispatch them to your vehicle during the chosen window.
  • You’ll receive an order confirmation and updates when a driver is assigned.
  • Your driver follows your instructions to locate the vehicle quickly and accurately.
  • If something is unclear (vehicle not found, access blocked), we may message/call through the app to resolve it fast.
  • We aim to provide a smooth arrival by using your address, pin, and notes—clear notes = faster service.
4) Prepare the gas cap area (ajar + accessible) Important
To keep service quick and hands-off, your gas cap/door must be unlocked and accessible at the time of refueling.
  • Please ensure the fuel door is unlocked (some vehicles lock the fuel door with the doors).
  • If your vehicle requires an interior release lever/button, please open the fuel door ahead of time.
  • Clear any obstacles so the driver can safely access the gas cap area (snow, cargo, tight parking, etc.).
  • If the cap/door is not accessible, service may be delayed or may need to be rescheduled.
5) Refueling completed + gas cap secured Service
Your driver refuels your vehicle according to your order and then re-secures everything before leaving the site.
  • The driver confirms the correct vehicle and fuel grade before fueling.
  • Refueling is performed carefully and professionally. We keep it neat and efficient.
  • After fueling, the driver tightens and secures the gas cap and closes the fuel door when applicable.
  • If the driver encounters an issue (blocked access, mismatched fuel type, etc.), they will contact you through the app.
6) Final payment is processed Checkout
Once fueling is complete, the final total is processed using the payment method on file.
  • Your final total reflects the fuel delivered plus any applicable fees.
  • Subscribers: service fee waived (your membership benefits apply automatically).
  • One-time orders: include the $15 service fee.
  • You’ll receive a confirmation once payment is successful and the order is completed.
7) Completion notification + digital receipt Receipt
You’ll get a completion alert and a digital receipt for your records—easy for personal budgeting or business tracking.
  • The app notifies you when the fueling is finished and the vehicle is secured.
  • Your receipt includes key details like fuel type, delivery time, and your total paid.
  • Receipts are sent stored digitally so you can reference them anytime.

Helpful tips:
• Add specific location notes (lot name, nearest business, parking spot number) for faster service.
• If your fuel door requires an interior release, open it before the window begins.
• Keep the gas cap area clear and accessible so the driver can complete the job quickly.
