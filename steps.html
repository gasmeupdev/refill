How It Works

Place an order in the Gas Me Up app and we’ll dispatch a driver to refuel your vehicle at your selected location. Follow the steps below to make sure everything goes smoothly.

1 Create your order (Subscriber or One-Time)

Open the app, create your account, and choose either a Subscriber plan or a One-Time Refill. Subscribers get the $15 service fee waived on refills, while One-Time Refills include a $15 service fee.

Tip: Subscribers are best if you refill regularly and want the service fee waived each time.
2 Select fuel type and time window

Choose your fuel grade (Regular / Mid / Premium) and select a refueling time window. Confirm the vehicle and the exact address/parking location where the vehicle will be when the driver arrives.

Tip: Add helpful notes like “parked behind building” or “use side lot entrance.”
3 Driver assignment and dispatch

Once your order is confirmed, a Gas Me Up driver is assigned and dispatched. You’ll receive app updates so you know when your driver is on the way and when they arrive.

Tip: Keep your phone nearby in case the driver needs a quick clarification.
4 Prepare your vehicle (gas cap accessible)

Before the driver arrives, please make sure your gas cap is ajar and easily accessible. If the fuel door is locked or blocked, the driver may not be able to complete the refill.

Tip: If your vehicle has a capless system, ensure the fuel door is unlocked and reachable.
5 Refueling and secure the gas cap

Your driver will refuel your vehicle according to your selected order details. After refueling is complete, the driver will secure your gas cap (and close the fuel door when applicable).

Tip: For safety, please keep the area around the vehicle clear while fueling is in progress.
6 Finalize charges and process payment

Once fueling is complete, we calculate your final total based on the fuel delivered, your plan type (Subscriber vs One-Time), and any applicable fees. Your payment method on file is then processed securely.

Tip: Make sure your payment method is up to date to avoid delays.
7 Completion notification and digital receipt

You’ll receive a completion notification once the order is finished. A digital receipt is generated and sent for your records, including key details like fuel type, quantity, and total charge.

Tip: You can view past receipts anytime inside the app.
