How It Works

Step-by-Step

  • Choose your fill-up option: Refill now (one-time request or to start a subscription) or Subscriber refill (for active subscriptions).
  • Enter your contact, car, and location information.
  • Select the time and date you’d like your car fueled.
  • Ensure your gas cap door is unlocked, open, or left ajar. If inaccessible, the driver will wait 5 minutes before the service is forfeited.
  • We bring gas to you. You'll receive updates on your driver’s estimated arrival and refill progress.
  • Your payment is processed securely via Stripe. One-time fill-ups incur a $15 fee plus gas; subscribers pay only for gas (monthly $25 fee billed separately). Receipts are provided both digitally and physically.

Additional Notes

  • If your vehicle is inaccessible due to traffic, parking, or other barriers, our driver will text you with options to reschedule or cancel at no charge.
  • Gas prices adjust daily based on local station rates. You'll be charged based on gallons dispensed multiplied by the day’s price (e.g., 10 gal x $3.50 = $35).
