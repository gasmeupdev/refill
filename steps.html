How It Works

Order fuel in minutes. Our drivers come to you—home, work, or wherever your vehicle is parked.

Step 1 Sign up in the app and choose Subscriber or a One-Time Refill.

One-Time Refill: includes a $15 service fee.
Subscribers: the $15 service fee is waived.
Step 2 Select your fuel type and choose your preferred refueling time.
Step 3 A Gas Me Up driver will be assigned and dispatched to your location.
Step 4 Please make sure your gas cap is ajar and accessible for our driver.
Step 5 After refueling is complete, your driver will secure your gas cap.
Step 6 We process your final payment (including fuel + any applicable fees).
Step 7 You’ll receive a completion notification, and we’ll generate a digital receipt for your records.
