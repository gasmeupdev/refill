How It Works

Place a fuel request online through our easy-to-use form.
A Gas Me Up driver is dispatched to your vehicle’s location.
Your vehicle’s gas cap must be unlocked or accessible for fueling.
The driver fills your vehicle with the requested amount of fuel.
Your vehicle is fueled safely and securely.
Payment is processed automatically through Stripe.
You receive a confirmation notification when fueling is complete.
