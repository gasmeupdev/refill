How It Works

Gas Me Up brings fuel directly to your vehicle. Place an order in the app, pick your fuel and time window, and our driver arrives to refuel your car safely and conveniently—then you receive a digital receipt.

Plans & fees

Subscriber

Service fee waived

Best for frequent refuels. Subscriber orders automatically apply your benefits and waive the $15 service fee so checkout stays fast and simple.

One-Time Refill

$15 service fee

Perfect for occasional use. One-time refills include a $15 service fee added to your final order total, plus the cost of fuel delivered.

Step-by-step
1

Sign up and choose your plan

Create your account, add your vehicle, and choose between a subscriber plan or a one-time refill.

  • Subscriber: service fee is waived on eligible subscriber orders.
  • One-time refill: includes a $15 service fee added to your total.
  • Add your vehicle details (make/model) and confirm the correct fuel type.
  • Add a payment method so checkout is quick and secure.
  • Optional: Add notes (gate code, “spot #22,” “parked behind building,” etc.).
2

Select your fuel grade + time window

Choose the fuel type you need and the delivery window that works best (same-day or scheduled windows as available).

  • Select your fuel grade (Regular / Mid / Premium) based on your vehicle’s recommendation.
  • Confirm the refuel location and add clear instructions for finding your vehicle.
  • Add helpful details like parking level, spot number, or nearest entrance.
  • Make sure the location is safe and legal for a quick service stop during your selected window.
3

Driver assigned + dispatched

Once your order is confirmed, we assign a Gas Me Up driver and dispatch them to your vehicle.

  • You’ll receive order updates when a driver is assigned and en route.
  • Drivers follow your notes to locate the vehicle quickly and complete service within the window.
  • If something is unclear (vehicle not found, access blocked), we may message/call through the app.
4

Make your gas cap accessible

To keep things fast and hands-off, your fuel door and gas cap area must be unlocked and reachable.

  • Ensure your fuel door is unlocked (some vehicles lock the fuel door with the doors).
  • If your vehicle uses an interior release, open the fuel door ahead of time.
  • Clear obstacles (snow/ice, tight parking, cargo blocking access) so the driver can reach the cap safely.
  • If the cap/door is not accessible, service may be delayed or may need to be rescheduled.
5

We refuel, then secure your gas cap

Your driver confirms the correct vehicle and fuel grade, refuels your car, then secures everything before leaving.

  • Driver verifies vehicle + fuel grade before beginning service.
  • Refueling is performed carefully and professionally.
  • After fueling, the driver tightens and secures the gas cap and closes the fuel door when applicable.
  • If an issue is found (blocked access, mismatched fuel type), the driver will contact you through the app.
6

Final payment is processed

Once fueling is complete, we process the final total using the payment method on file.

  • Your final total reflects the fuel delivered plus any applicable fees.
  • Subscribers: service fee waived automatically.
  • One-time orders: include the $15 service fee.
  • You’ll receive confirmation once payment is successful and the order is completed.
7

Completion notification + digital receipt

We notify you when the job is done and provide a digital receipt for your records.

  • The app notifies you when service is finished and the vehicle is secured.
  • Your receipt includes key details like fuel type, delivery time, and total paid.
  • Receipts are stored digitally so you can reference them anytime.
  • If you ever need support, your receipt helps us quickly identify the order and assist you.
Quick prep checklist

Add clear location details

Spot number, garage level, nearest entrance, or a landmark helps your driver find your vehicle fast.

Unlock the fuel door

Some vehicles lock the fuel door with the doors. Make sure it’s unlocked during the service window.

Keep the gas cap area accessible

Clear snow/ice and avoid parking too close to walls so the driver can safely reach the cap.

Save your receipt automatically

You’ll receive a digital receipt after completion for personal or business tracking.
