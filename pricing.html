Pricing

Non-Subscriber Checkout

A $25 temporary hold is placed on your credit card at checkout to verify available funds. After fueling is complete, you’ll be charged a $15 service fee plus the cost of the gasoline. Note: We sell gas at cost — you pay exactly what we pay..

Subscribers

Subscribers pay a $25 recurring monthly fee and enjoy fuel delivery with no service fee. You’re only charged for the actual cost of the gasoline at each fill-up .

Become a Subscriber

Signing up is easy. When placing your first fuel order, simply select “Yes” when prompted to subscribe at checkout. Your $25 monthly subscription will begin with your first order and renew every 30 days thereafter.

Secure Payment Processing

All payments are securely managed by Stripe, Inc., a trusted and accredited third-party payment processor. Stripe handles all authorization, authentication, and payment execution, ensuring your information is safe and transactions are seamless.
