Pricing

Non‑Subscriber Checkout

A $25 temporary hold is placed on your card at checkout to verify funds. After fueling, you’ll be charged a $15 service fee plus the cost of gasoline.

Subscribers

Subscribers pay a $25 recurring monthly fee and enjoy fuel delivery with no service fee. You’re only charged for the actual cost of the gasoline at each fill‑up.

Become a Subscriber

Signing up is easy. At your first fuel order simply select “Yes” when prompted to subscribe at checkout. Your $25 subscription begins with that order and renews every 30 days.

Secure Payment Processing

All payments are securely managed by Stripe, Inc., a trusted third‑party payment processor. Stripe handles authorization, authentication, and payment execution, keeping your data safe.
