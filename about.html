Who We Are and What We Do

A group of local Duquesne University grduates came together and created Pittsburgh's newest startup, providing an on-demand gas delivery directly to your car. Join the #NoStationNation today. We're not wasting your time. Just Gassing You Up

How It Works

1. Book in Minutes

Using our website or mobile app, enter your contact info, vehicle details, location, fuel type, and payment method. For non-subscribers, we’ll place a $25 temporary hold to verify funds, but you won’t be charged until fueling is complete. For subscribers, you simply login with your phone and email address

2. Get Notified

Once you book, a driver confirms your exact delivery time within your chosen window. You’ll get a text 2 hours before your time slot with a precise fueling time.
Note: Bookings must be made at least 2 hours in advance.

3. Fuel Delivery

Make sure your car is unlocked and the gas cap is accessible when or shortly after the driver arrives. If the car is locked or the gas cap inaccessible the driver will text, email and call the customer. Our drivers will wait up to 5 minutes after that, they must leave and the service fee is non-refundable.

4. You're Gassed Up

  • We’ll lock your vehicle and secure the gas cap
  • Record the exact amount of fuel dispensed
  • Process your final payment

Easy, safe, and contactless. Your tank is full — and you didn’t lift a finger.
